Splatware Sets

Choose your desired amount of plates and cups. Splatware plates and cups are made using a durable high-firing Stafordshire stoneware clay, glazed and fired to vitrification temperatures, meaning they are food, dishwasher and microwave safe. The finish is reminiscent of traditional ateware or nerikomi ceramics, where the patterning is not a glaze effect or transfer - the clay itself is coloured all the way through, giving a unique depth of colour and finish. Patterns and colours will vary.
